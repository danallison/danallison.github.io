The term _wu wei_ is a Taoist concept that is often translated into English as _non-doing_ or _effortless action_. It conveys a sense of power through flow rather than force.

__The Universe__ indicates completion and satisfaction. The __9 of Wands__ indicates strength that comes from experience. Together there is a sense of equanimity, flow, and non-attachment to outcome.

The pursuit of a goal can unfold as a battle against opposing forces or it can unfold as a playful dance with ever changing circumstances. Letting go of the outcome can transform obstacles into a playground.

## Questions

* What battles are you fighting that you might choose to approach more playfully?
* How might releasing control increase your confidence?
* What wants to happen through your actions?
