# Stereo Tarot

__Stereo Tarot__ is the name I've given to the practice of synthesizing two tarot cards into a single card, like how stereoscopic vision is synthesized from two flat images. My plan is to make consecutive decks of 39 stereo cards each (78 base tarot cards = 39 pairs). I will continue this practice for as long as it feels alive.
